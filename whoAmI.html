Origins

November 26, 2000. That is the day I came into the world and for as long as I can remember I have always had a strong passion towards all technology. I was born and raised in the capatial of Alberta, Canada.. Edmonton. I grew up in the city and I will forever love the busy city life. I lived in Edmonton up until grade 6 where I went to a couple different schools such as St. Elizabeth and James Gibbons. With me being a city person I was not the happiest in grade 7 when we moved and I had to go to Pigeon Lake Regional School. That lasted a year and the rest of my childhood expierence was finished in High Prairie where I attended Prairie River Junior High School and later on E.W. Pratt High School. After highschool I quickly moevd back to the busy city of Edmonton to go to university. At first I was very undecided on what I wanted to go to school for and origionally I decided to go to Concordia University of Edmonton for a music degree. It took a little but but eventually I decided to stick with my long lasting passion of technology and persue an education in some form of computer science.
